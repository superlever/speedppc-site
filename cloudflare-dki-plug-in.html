Cloudflare DKI Plug-in

Make your Cloudflare powered website have Dynamic Keyword Insertion

Increase Your Website Conversion Rates

Turn your Cloudflare powered site into a high performing landing page machine.

Hey, if you really want to boost those conversion rates, you've got to make sure your website closely reflects what people are searching for. Trust me, it makes a difference.


You know what makes visitors happy? Seeing their exact search terms right there on the landing page. It's like a little "You've come to the right place!" sign, and it does wonders for your conversions.


But let's be real, making a separate page for every search query just isn't practical. That's where dynamic keyword insertion saves the day. With Cloudflare, you can add this nifty feature to your site without breaking a sweat.

Just set up Cloudflare with our code, pop the right token on your page, and put any keyword you want in the URL. Voilà! The keyword shows up in the designated spot on the page, whether you've got one or a million different ones – all on a single page.


Seriously, customizing your content has never been easier, thanks to Cloudflare and SpeedPPC.

Cloudflare DKI Features

A simple to use, but super powered solution to increase your conversion rates.

It's straight forward to get DKI going on your Cloudflare powered website! Some of our it's features include:

Use On Unlimited Sites

So long as your a SpeedPPC customer, you can use this solution on an unlimited number of your websites.

Integrates Easily with SpeedPPC

Just set up the URL in SpeedPPC to include the keyword dynamically and it will pass it directly into the landing page.

Unlimited Tokens Replaced

You can use as many tokens as you like so you can replace as many parts of the website as you like.

Modify the Case and Replace Hyphens

Within the token, you can change case which is perfect for when you need to start the sentence with a dynamic token.
