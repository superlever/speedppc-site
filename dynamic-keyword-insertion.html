What On Earth Is DKI? 

Alright marketing maestros, let's dive into the nitty-gritty of dynamic keyword insertion. First things first, what exactly is it?


Well, think of DKI as a personal shopper for your campaigns. It allows you to dynamically insert keywords into your page copy (including headlines and sub-headlines), making each page unique and relevant to the searcher's query. So, instead of creating one generic landing page that may or may not resonate with your target audience, DKI creates a custom page for each individual search.


But how does it actually work in your campaigns? It's simple. You set up DKI in your platform and include special tags in your copy where you want the keywords to appear. When someone searches for those keywords, the platform automatically replaces the tags with the actual keywords, creating a custom page.


For example, let's say you're a clothing retailer and you have an ad with the following copy: "Get ready for summer with our [keyword]!"

With DKI, if someone searches for "men's shorts" the ad will show as "Get ready for summer with our men's shorts!" See how that works?


So, say goodbye to generic ads and landing pages and hello to a more personalized and relevant experience for your target audience.

Benefits of Dynamic Keyword Insertion

Let's talk about the benefits of dynamic keyword insertion. 


First and foremost, DKI increases relevance and personalization for your target audience. No more generic pages that fail to grab their attention. With DKI, each page is unique and relevant to their search query, making them feel like you're speaking directly to them.


Not only does DKI increase relevance and personalization, it also leads to improved click-through rates (CTR). Your target audience is more likely to click on an a page that speaks directly to their search query, leading to a higher CTR for your campaigns.


And, let's talk about the ultimate goal of any campaign - conversions! With a higher CTR and relevance, it's no surprise that DKI leads to higher conversion rates. Your target audience is more likely to take action when they feel like the page was created specifically for them.


But, let's not forget about ROI. The increased relevance, CTR, and conversions all lead to better ad performance and ultimately, a higher return on investment for your campaigns.


How to Easily Implement Dynamic Keyword Insertion to improve landing pages? 

First, you'll need to set up it up in your chosen platform. This typically involves adding special tags in your copy where you want the keywords to appear, and then configuring the platform to automatically replace those tags with the actual keywords. The specifics will vary depending on the platform you're using, so be sure to check their support documentation for step-by-step instructions.


Many landing page services like Unbounce and Swipe Pages allows you to do this automatically.


SpeedPPC has created a Wordpress Plug-in and a Cloudflare integration to also achieve the same outcome. This is available on all Solo+ and Agency plans.


Now that you've got DKI set up, let's talk about best practices for using it effectively. Firstly, don't go overboard with the keywords within your PPC ad copy. Stick to 1-2 relevant keywords per ad, and make sure they fit naturally into the ad copy. Secondly, ensure that the landing page is relevant to the keywords in the ad. A hyper-targeted ad is only as good as the landing page it leads to, so make sure they complement each other.


You see, a hyper-targeted ad is only as good as the landing page it leads to. Imagine this scenario - a potential customer clicks on your ad because it's relevant to their search query, but then they land on a page that has nothing to do with it. Not only will they quickly bounce, but they'll also have a negative impression of your brand.


That's why it's crucial to ensure your landing page is relevant to the keywords in your ad. You can create a custom ad for each individual search, so it only makes sense that the landing page should be just as personalized. This will not only increase the likelihood of conversions, but it'll also provide a seamless experience for your target audience.
