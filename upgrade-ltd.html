Have questions? We've answers.

Do I get all these new feature upgrades with my LTD Plan?

Yes, you get all the features linked to the Pro plan (and most of you get more). A few smaller features are linked to the Agency plan, like exporting Landing Page Analysis and LTV Funnel maps.


The DKI WordPress Plugin and Cloudflare DKI are brand-new products.


If these are of interest to you to get, you can upgrade to the new Tier 4 & 5 plans. 

What are the new Tier 4 and 5 LTD plans?

This is in response to people wanting more lifetime AI and Research credits. So we’ve added some new Tiers with higher limits. They also get a bonus deal for the new DKI products as well.


As a deal sweetener, we've also added a bonus 1-hour strategy session video call with anyone who buys Tier 4 & Tier 5.

My original plan said I had 1000 lifetime credits; now, I have 1.5 million. What gives?

So previously, we didn’t have any AI stuff at all. Just research reports. Now we have the ability to run a report on a single keyword. So we weren't going to charge .002 of a credit. So we just increased everything up so we could use whole numbers.


We also thought we'd do something nice and more or less increase your credits by 50% and more or less reset you.

How do you mean you more or less reset my credits back to full again?

The long and short of it is that we didn't pro-rata the credits. We just left them as they were. 


So even if you had used 500 of your 1000 credits, we haven’t made you have 750,000 of your 1.5M credits. We just left it at 1,499,500 instead. We want you guys to be super happy and advocates for SpeedPPC.

Can I buy more credits later?

We used only to have the option where you would have to move to a monthly credit plan once you had used all of your credits. However, we have improved that, so you can now buy credits as you need. These are $30 for 100,000 credits. The monthly plan is no longer available.
