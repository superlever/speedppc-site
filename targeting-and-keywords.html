HOW TO STOP YOUR PPC CAMPAIGNS BEING A CASH DUMPSTER FIRE

Stop Bad Targeting

Make more money from every campaign.

Why you'll fail without SpeedPPC

You'll bid on straight up bad keywords

Over 50% of the keywords that people use are terrible. They are either informational or navigational and will never turn into a sale and waste your cash.

You'll miss great keywords that generate sales

There are so many keywords that you are currently not bidding on that you should be. So you're effectively losing money by not targeting those people.

Why you'll win with SpeedPPC

Filter your keywords to find the ones that convert

We analyze your keywords and allow you to filter them into commercial, transactional, informational and navigational. You can then just focus on using the one's that convert (hint: it's the first two).

Discover your competitors winning keywords

Use the competitor analysis (interrogate competitor and competitor comparison) reports to discover what keywords are working for your competitors that you are not using. Let them spend their budget finding the winning keywords.

Find new converting keywords

Brainstorm new keywords for your niche that you may not have thought of. Then filter by keyword intent to make sure you only use the ones that will actually convert.

Expand on the keywords that are already working

Got keywords that are already working? Expand on those keywords to go deeper and again, filter what is found by keyword intent to make sure you only use the ones that will convert.

Use AI Clustering to thematically group keywords

Got a huge list of keywords from other tools, but don't know how to sort them? Use our AI tool to thematically sort them into groups so you can create ads that match.

Create negative keyword sets

What you negative match is as important as what you keywords you bid on. Use our negative keyword sets or create your own.
