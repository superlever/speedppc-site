HOW TO STOP YOUR PPC CAMPAIGNS BEING A CASH DUMPSTER FIRE

Your Campaigns Are Weak

Low ad relevance is killing your campaigns. Stop painting a Picasso with a paint roller.

How bad campaign structure will lose you money

Generic campaigns don't work

So many people run campaigns with generic ads with their fingers crossed hoping that they will work. They don't. The ads that work are the ones that perfectly match what people are looking for in their search query, not some lazy generic billboard ad.

Low Click Through Rates (CTR)

If the ad isn't relevant or compelling, people just won't click on it. This then has lots of negative side effects like lower ad positions, higher CPCs and lower volume. Ultimately the end result is you losing more money.

Low Quality Scores

A poorly built campaign with low relevance between the keyword, ad and landing page is assigned a low Quality Score. It is extremely difficult for any campaign to be successful with low Quality Scores.

Higher Costs

Your badly structured, weak campaigns are punished by making you pay more for every click.

How SpeedPPC creates winning campaigns

Incredible relevance = incredible performance

The holy grail is when the ad and landing page perfectly matches what someone is searching for. So every product and every service needs to have a matching ad and landing page.

SpeedPPC helps you do this easily and at scale with its incredible campaign builder. What used to take half a day now takes you 2 minutes.

Improves your Quality Score

In Google Ads, a high Quality Score can lower your CPCs and give your ad a better position on the search results page (so you'll get more clicks).

Quality Scores are determined by factors like the relevance of the keywords, the relevance and quality of the ad copy, and the relevance and quality of the landing page.

SpeedPPC helps you increase this relevance by creating more tightly structured campaigns based around single keywords or themes.

Increases your Click Through Rate (CTR)

Google rewards high CTR as it is an indicator of user engagement and relevance, and this rewards campaigns with higher ad visibility, lower CPCs and better overall performance.

With more relevant ads, you'll get more people wanting to click them. As a result, you'll get more volume at lower cost.

Lowers your Cost Per Click (CPC)

With higher CTR and Quality Score, you will be rewarded with lower cost per clicks. Allowing you to increase volumes and profits at the same time.

Increases your conversion rates

When you create a great structure that allows for keyword level URL's, you can create landing pages that convert better because they are more relevant. Throw in Dynamic Keyword Insertion, and you have a conversion machine!

Increases click volumes

With improved Quality Scores and Click Through Rates you will get more clicks for the same budget.

Saves you a massive amount of time

Creating very narrow and targeted campaigns normally take an annoyingly long amount of time. Forget about that with SpeedPPC. What would normally take you hours or even days, takes you minutes.
