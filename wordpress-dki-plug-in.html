Wordpress DKI Plug-in

Make your Wordpress website have Dynamic Keyword Insertion

Increase Your WordPress Conversion Rates

Turn your WordPress site into a high performing landing page machine.

If you want to get maximum conversions, you need to make your website match what people are looking as closely as possible.

People love to see on the landing page the very query they typed into the search engine. They know they are in the right place and your conversions go up accordingly.

But to create a new page for every search query is impossible. Except when you have dynamic keyword insertion. Simply install the plugin-in, insert the token into the page and pass whatever you like in the URL and it will appear in that spot on the page. Whether it's one or a million different keywords. It's just one page.

It's so simple to do with this plug-in.

Wordpress DKI Features

A simple to use, but super powered plug-in making your conversions dreams come true.

It's super easy to install and get going on your WordPress website. Some of our it's features include:

Use On Unlimited WordPress Sites

So long as your a SpeedPPC customer, you can use this plug-in on an unlimited number of your websites.

Integrates Easily with SpeedPPC

Just set up the URL in SpeedPPC to include the keyword dynamically and it will pass it directly into the landing page.

Unlimited Tokens Replaced

You can use as many tokens as you like so you can replace as many parts of the website as you like.

Modify the Case and Replace Hyphens

Within the token, you can change case which is perfect for when you need to start the sentence with a dynamic token.
